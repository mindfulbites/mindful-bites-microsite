Yaco-What?

Never heard of Yacon? That's because we're one of the first to include this awesome root into our snacks. But we haven't invented it! Yacon has actually been around for hundreds of years in the Andes mountain.

Yaco-Immuno

Okay, that's not a real word. But it should be! Yacon is full of properties which boost immunity. It is also an excellent prebiotic which helps the good gut bacteria. And you know what the experts say about a healthy gut (seems pretty crucial for a lower risk of of diabetes, obesity and improved brain function). Hence, Yaco-Immuno.

For The Throne

The Incas used to drink Yacon as a tea to help with toilet troubles. Rich in fibre, the syrup is proven to help with bowel movements and relieve constipation.
