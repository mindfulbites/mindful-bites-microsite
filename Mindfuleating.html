MINDFUL EATING

What if we told you that no, you do not need to become a Tibetan monk to eat mindfully? No weird techniques, no voodoo practices and definitely no incense. Just you in the present moment, intentionally and non-judgmentally, fully aware in the now.

Maybe it’s a technique, maybe it's an art form but whatever it is, just have some fun! If you’re going to take a break from your day to have a snack, let’s make that moment counts, after all it is your break!

So let us all take 4 steps to get there. 1 step and one bite at a time- there is no rush! Remember, it is your break after all!


INTENTION

Are you hungry?
Are you actually hungry?
Maybe you are bored, upset or somebody has annoyed you. Let’s sit with this for a moment whilst the world keeps happening, this is your moment.
So, your body is telling you that you are hungry.

ATTENTION

Great! So you are hungry and you’ve reached for food.
Wait…That looks really good!
Tell us about it, we really want to know.
What awesome food are you eating?
Look at the colours, look at it’s shape.
What does it smell like?
Does it have a particular smell?
Does it remind you of a place you’ve visited or maybe somebody?
Have you noticed the texture of your food?
Is it crunchy?
Crumbly?
Maybe even a bit melty?
We know there is noise around you, and stress and that deadline, but what the heck, this is your moment!
This is still an awesome moment to experience great flavours and captivating textures.

GRATITUDE

Hold this little bite, your little bite, such a small bite.
And yet it has a story, just like you.
The story of people who farmed the ingredients, picked the nuts, milled the grains,
The story of the people who carefully handle those ingredients to create this little composition of flavours.
The story of the people who neatly stack the shelves for you to have this mindful bite.
So here’s to you, little bite, for providing positive nutrition, for taking us back to the land that gives us more even when we give less.
And thank you for connecting us with awesome people who might not know how awesome they truly are.
Fiew! That was deep…But what joy it has brought into the present moment.

PLEASURE

Smile, laugh and laugh harder if you want.
This is your moment, enjoy it with full heart.
Eating is pleasure.
Enjoy your bite and enjoy a bit more.
This is your break.
This is your moment.
Oh dear! Look at your watch.
What time is it? The time is now.
But look again. Yes, it is now, again.
